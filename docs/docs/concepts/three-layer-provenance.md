# Three-Layer Provenance

GNS-AIP models the AI supply chain as three distinct layers.

## The Three Layers

| Layer | Role | Example | Artifact |
|-------|------|---------|----------|
| **Creator** | Built the model | Anthropic, OpenAI, Meta | Model origin hash |
| **Deployer** | Provisions and configures agents | Your company | AgentManifest |
| **Principal** | Authorizes specific actions | Human user | DelegationCert |

## Why This Matters

**The Creator has no regulatory obligation to give its models identity.** The Deployer, by contrast, has direct regulatory exposure: EU AI Act compliance deadlines, GDPR accountability, FINMA audit requirements.

**Don't sell to Layer 1. Sell to Layer 2.** Layer 1 has no pain. Layer 2 has compliance deadlines, audit requirements, and liability exposure. Pain is where budgets open.

## The Path to Layer 1

When enough deployers use GNS-AIP, the AI labs will integrate at the model level because their enterprise customers demanded it. The path to Layer 1 runs through Layer 2, not around it.
