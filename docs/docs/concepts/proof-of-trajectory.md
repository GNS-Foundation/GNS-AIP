# Proof-of-Trajectory

**You are not your iris. You are your trajectory.**

Proof-of-Trajectory (PoT) is the foundational innovation of the GNS Protocol. Instead of biometric scanning (like WorldCoin's iris scan), PoT proves humanity through movement patterns.

## How It Works

Real humans move through physical space in patterns that are:
- **Consistent** — daily routines, commute patterns, familiar locations
- **Unpredictable** — no two days are exactly alike
- **Unfakeable** — generating realistic movement over weeks/months requires a physical body

Smartphones collect cryptographically-signed location breadcrumbs at the point of occurrence. Over time, these breadcrumbs form a trajectory that proves the device (and its owner) is a real human moving through real space.

## For AI Agents

AI agents don't move through physical space, but they operate in digital territory. GNS-AIP adapts PoT for agents through:

- **Virtual breadcrumbs** — operation metadata signed at execution time
- **H3 territorial binding** — agents are bound to geographic cells
- **Operational trajectory** — consistent patterns over time build compliance score
